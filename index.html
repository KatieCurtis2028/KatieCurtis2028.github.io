About Me

I am pursuing my PhD in kinesiology with an emphasis in exercise physiology at Michigan State University. I have worked in the Sports Science department at MSU for the last four years. I coordinate the Catapult devices for MSU Volleyball. I also teach in the Kinesiology department in addition to my work with Sports Science.
