About Me

I am a second-year graduate student pursuing a Ph.D. in kinesiology with an emphasis in exercise physiology at Michigan State University. I earned her M.S in kinesiology at Michigan State and my B.S in exercise science at Winona State Univeirsity. I am now a teaching assistant in the department of kinesiology under the guidance of Dr. Karin Pfeiffer. Additionally, I work in the Sports Science department at Michigan State under the mentorship of Dr. Bill Burghardt. My research centers on wearable technology, athlete workload monitoring, and talent development.
